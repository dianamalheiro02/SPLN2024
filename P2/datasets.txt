    IMDb Movie Reviews: A dataset of movie reviews labeled as positive or negative 
sentiment based on the review text.
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/lakshmi25npathi/imdb-dataset-of-50k-movie-reviews

    Twitter Sentiment Analysis Dataset: A collection of tweets labeled with sentiment, 
often classified as positive, negative, or neutral.
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/crowdflower/twitter-airline-sentiment

    Stanford Sentiment Treebank: This dataset provides sentiment labels for 
phrases in movie reviews on a fine-grained scale.
https://nlp.stanford.edu/sentiment/treebank.html

Financial News Sentiment Analysis: Datasets focused on sentiment analysis 
in financial news articles, often used for stock market prediction.
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ankurzing/sentiment-analysis-for-financial-news

    MPQA Subjectivity Lexicon: This lexicon contains words labeled with subjectivity and 
polarity information. It's often used in sentiment analysis and opinion mining tasks.
https://mpqa.cs.pitt.edu/lexicons/subj_lexicon/

    Bing Liu's Lexicon: Similar to the Opinion Lexicon, this lexicon contains a list 
of words labeled as either positive or negative based on their sentiment polarity.
https://juliasilge.github.io/tidytext/reference/sentiments.html
